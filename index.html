About Me

I am a software engineer with a focus toward responsive, accessible and efficient design. I am an analytical thinker who likes to know how and why things work the way that they do.

Prior to becoming a developer, I spent the last 13 years as a Fleet Manager in agriculture and oil & gas. During this time, I managed as many as 700 drivers and 4,000 assets with an annual budget of $35MM. In 2019 I consulted with Hitachi on the development of the first AI Fleet Management software to maximize productivity and reduce unexpected downtime. In 2021 I co-authored Oklahoma House Bill 2325 to eliminate the need for pilot cars for retail implement dealers.

These experiences gave me the skills in day-to-day business operations, budgeting, regulatory compliance, and data analysis but none of it would have been possible without teamwork, collaboration, and processes.

The tech resources and community are such a blessing and I enjoy being a contributing part of the tech community. Thanks for checking out my page!
